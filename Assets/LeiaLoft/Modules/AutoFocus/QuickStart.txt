	Leia Auto Focus Add-on

	Description:
  	Provides functionality for automating the LeiaCamera's convergence distance.

	Quick start:
  1) Attach LeiaAutoFocus.cs to a GameObject with a LeiaCamera component.

  2) Choose a tracking type.
     Target:
     	Drag a GameObject to the Target field.
     	Convergence will be set on the Target's position. 
     Auto:
     	A ray will be cast from the LeiaCamera's center.
     	Convergence will be set to the hit position of the ray and a Collider.
